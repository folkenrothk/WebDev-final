Escape w/ Me: The Road to Absolute Perfection

Stardew Valley

Stardew Valley, though known for escapism, cannot escape from societal defaults and problems. I participated in the creation of a new digital identity, Tru_Gamer. This brand is supposed to be interpreted as cute through the rounded edges but slightly masculine with the cool colors. This art also portrays an important root in digital media as everything is outlined by browser/website frames. I chose these pieces to represent the desired aesthetics, masculine/male majority, and be firmly grounded in digital culture. This piece does not include voice-over commentary nor additional video feeds. I chose not to add these pieces as their absence highlights the level of connection between the creator and viewer. This disrupts the para-social relationships existing between viewers and creators as there is no visual or auditory connection. This piece– the video– itself is still a commentary on gaming defaults. From the start of the game with the avatar creation to resource extraction and the optimization of gameplay influenced by streaming content.
